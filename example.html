Asset Depreciation Calculator


Example Calculation - 01

I have taken the Depreciation Calculation Date as 01st – Nov -2023


If the asset was purchased on 10th Feb 2022, what is the depreciated value of the asset now?

The purchase value of the asset = 9800 (irrespective of the currency)


First, calculate the "Used Life" in days:

Used Life = Depreciation Calculation Date - Purchase Date/invoice date

Used Life = 01st Nov 2023 - 10th Feb 2022

Used Life = 629 days


Now, calculate the "Remaining Life" in days:

Remaining Life = (3 years * 365 days) - Used Life

Remaining Life = (3 * 365) - 629

Remaining Life = 1,096 - 629

Remaining Life = 467 days


Next, calculate the "Per Day Depreciation Charge":

Per Day Depreciation Charge = Purchase Value / Life

Per Day Depreciation Charge = 9800 / 1096

Per Day Depreciation Charge = 8.94


Now, calculate the "Depreciation Charge" for the used period:

Depreciation Charge = Used Life * Per Day Depreciation Charge

Depreciation Charge = 629 * 8.94

Depreciation Charge = 5614.26


Finally, calculate the "Book Value" of the asset:

Book Value = Purchase Value - Depreciation Charge

Book Value = 9800 - 5614.26

Book Value = 4185.74


So, if the asset was purchased on 10th Feb 2022 and the depreciation calculation date is 01st Nov 2023, the depreciated value of the asset would be approximately 4185.74.

(However, HR can change this value)

Example Calculation - 02

If the asset was purchased on 03rd Jan 2023, what is the depreciated value of the asset now?

The purchase value of the asset = 6300 (irrespective of the currency)


Calculate the "Used Life" in days:

Used Life = Depreciation Calculation Date - Purchase Date

Used Life = 01st Nov 2023 - 03rd Jan 2023

Used Life = 303 days


Calculate the "Remaining Life" in days:

Remaining Life = (3 years * 365 days/year) - Used Life

Remaining Life = (3 * 365) - 303

Remaining Life = 1,095 - 303

Remaining Life = 792 days


Calculate the "Per Day Depreciation Charge":

Per Day Depreciation Charge = Purchase Value / Life

Per Day Depreciation Charge = 6300 / 1095

Per Day Depreciation Charge ≈ 5.75


Calculate the "Depreciation Charge" for the used period:

Depreciation Charge = Used Life * Per Day Depreciation Charge

Depreciation Charge = 303 * 5.75

Depreciation Charge ≈ 1748.25


Calculate the "Book Value" of the asset:

Book Value = Purchase Value - Depreciation Charge

Book Value = 6300 - 1748.25

Book Value ≈ 4551.75


So, if the asset was purchased on 03rd Jan 2023 and the depreciation calculation date is 01st Nov 2023, the depreciated value of the asset would be approximately 4551.75.
